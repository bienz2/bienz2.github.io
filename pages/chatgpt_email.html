AI-Generated Emails

Planning to use ChatGPT to write an email for you? Please rethink that idea.

Let's say I give ChatGPT a simple prompt asking for an email letting a professor know I will be missing class due to an illness. Instead of responding with a consise and direct message, ChatGPT outputs the longest history known to man, yet it says very little. You can ask it to be more consise and that helps a little... But really, you need to ask it to be less weird to end up with an email that a student would actually send. At this point, you would have saved a lot of time just writing this email yourself.
